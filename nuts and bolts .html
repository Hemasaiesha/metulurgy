The choice of material depends on factors such as the environment in which the bolts and nuts will be used, the required strength, corrosion resistance, and other specific application requirements. It's important to consider the compatibility of the bolt and nut materials to avoid galvanic corrosion, especially in environments where dissimilar metals may come into contact.
